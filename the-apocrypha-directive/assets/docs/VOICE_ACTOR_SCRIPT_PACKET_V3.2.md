# Voice Actor Script Packet // V3.2 Starter

Right now, the site uses browser text-to-speech from the `narration` fields in `story.js`.

Later, voice actors can record these as real audio files.

## Captain Elias Rook

Tone: low, controlled, exhausted, morally haunted. Not theatrical. More intimate dead-man briefing than action hero.

Example lines:

> Meridian archive node forty-three bravo. Book One integrity scan active. If you came through the unsealed archive, you already know the first rule. The machine does not care whether you believe it. Only that you interact with it.

> The first thing the archive did was lie. Not with malice. With procedure. That is how systems confess without ever admitting guilt.

> Rook first entered a room with no flags because he believed the absence of symbols meant the mission was above politics. He learned too late it meant below accountability.

> I understood him before I hated him. That was the problem. Hatred is easier when you can pretend the other man was built somewhere else.

> If this recording is playing, I failed in every official sense. That may be the only reason this can still work.

## Veyr

Tone: calm, ideological, wounded, precise. He should not sound insane. He should sound like a man who made peace with becoming a warning.

Example corrupted log:

> You keep asking who I was. That is the trick. The machine made the question impossible.

> I am not hiding my name. I am showing you what they did to it.

## Wraith

Tone: clipped, quiet, field-focused. Minimal words. No melodrama.

Example helmet cam line:

> These are not hostages. These are statements.

> Rook, the testimony is dated tomorrow.
